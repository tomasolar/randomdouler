# Cultural Funding Landscape

*These institutions don't fund operations — they fund projects. Keep this in mind.*

---

## Local — Marbella & Málaga

**Ayuntamiento de Marbella**
The city council supports cultural festivals, exhibitions, community art programs, and music events. Good entry point for visibility and local legitimacy.

**Diputación de Málaga**
Provincial government. Supports art exhibitions, cultural heritage, and regional artistic development. Often involves partnerships with local municipalities.

---

## Regional — Andalusia

**Junta de Andalucía**
The regional government's cultural departments support contemporary art programs, cultural innovation, and community-based activities. Requires collaboration with recognized cultural partners.

---

## Nordic Network

**Taike — Arts Promotion Centre Finland**
Supports Finnish artists working internationally. Relevant for exhibitions abroad and project-based international work. Does not fund private business operations.

**Frame Contemporary Art Finland**
Promotes Finnish contemporary art internationally. Supports exhibitions, institutional collaborations, and international networking for artists and curators.

**Finnish Cultural Institute in Madrid**
Focused on cultural exchange between Finland and Spain. Strong fit for exhibitions, Nordic programming, and Finnish-Spanish artistic connections.

**Nordic Culture Point**
Supports cultural cooperation between Nordic countries and international partners. Particularly relevant for multi-Nordic collaborations.

---

## European

**Creative Europe (EU)**
Large-scale collaborative cultural projects across Europe. Only becomes relevant if Random Corner evolves into a broader platform with multiple institutional partners.

---

## Strategic Note

These programs support *specific projects* — exhibitions, artist residencies, cultural exchange initiatives, collaborative productions. They are not a financial base for the studio.

The right time to engage them is when Random Corner has established its identity and has a concrete project proposal to offer. Not before.

The primary drivers remain: *artistic production, music gatherings, community.*
