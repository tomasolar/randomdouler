# Content Calendar

*Post consistently. Post honestly. Don't overthink it.*

---

## Weekly Rhythm

| Day | Content Idea |
|---|---|
| Monday | Studio shot — something in progress on the wall or table |
| Wednesday | Short video — 15–30 seconds of the space, music, or painting process |
| Thursday | Pre-event story: *"Tonight. Studio. Music."* |
| Friday | Post-event: one photo or reel from last night's jam |

That's 3–4 posts a week. Consistent beats impressive.

---

## Content Categories

### The Work
- New paintings or works in progress
- Detail shots — texture, colour, scale
- A finished piece with one sentence about it

### The Studio
- Wide shots of the space as it evolves
- Morning light, evening atmosphere
- The messy reality of a working studio

### The Music
- Candid moments from jam nights
- Musician portraits — who's here tonight
- Short video clips of sessions (30–60 seconds, no production needed)

### The People
- Visitors in the space (ask permission)
- Conversations — a quote, a thought
- Behind-the-scenes of setup

---

## Captions

Keep them short. One thought. No hashtag walls.

Examples:
> *Thursday again.*

> *New work. Still drying.*

> *Good night in the studio.*

> *Some evenings you don't want to go home.*

## Instagram Stories

Use stories for the immediate, in-the-moment content:
- Tonight's jam night invite
- Something happening right now in the studio
- Quick polls or questions to the audience

Stories don't need to be polished. That's the point.

---

## Photography Notes

- Natural light where possible
- Candid over posed
- Low-fi phone video often works better than produced content for this kind of space
- Document everything — you'll be glad you did
