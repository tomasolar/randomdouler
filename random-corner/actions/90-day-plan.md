# 90-Day Plan

*The goal of these 90 days is not growth. It's foundation.*

---

## Weeks 1–4 — Activate

The studio becomes a functioning cultural space. Not perfect — active.

**Studio**
- [ ] Finalize the core layout — artwork placement, lighting, visitor flow
- [ ] Ensure a visible body of work is always present for visitors
- [ ] The space should feel like someone works here

**Art**
- [ ] Complete key artworks currently in progress
- [ ] Photograph completed works properly for Instagram and website

**Jam Nights**
- [ ] Launch Thursday Jam Nights
- [ ] Identify 3–5 musicians as the core regular crew
- [ ] Test the technical setup — sound, space, flow

**Network**
- [ ] Invite friends, local contacts, and musicians to visit
- [ ] Start building a small circle of regular faces

---

## Weeks 5–8 — Establish Rhythm

People start associating Thursday with Random Corner.

**Jam Nights**
- [ ] Host consistently — every Thursday, no exceptions
- [ ] Encourage musicians to bring friends and collaborators
- [ ] Keep the format open and relaxed — no set lists, no tickets

**Studio Visits**
- [ ] Begin inviting potential collectors and culturally curious individuals
- [ ] Let them experience the studio naturally — don't pitch, show

**Community**
- [ ] Strengthen relationships with the musicians and creatives already coming
- [ ] The atmosphere should start to feel like *a regular thing*

---

## Weeks 9–12 — Expand

The network begins to extend beyond the original circle.

**Collectors**
- [ ] Host informal studio visits with individuals interested in art and culture
- [ ] These are conversations, not sales meetings

**Cultural Gatherings**
- [ ] Continue Thursdays
- [ ] Consider one small themed evening combining art and music — something with a reason to come

**Local Connections**
- [ ] Build relationships with artists, musicians, and cultural figures in Marbella
- [ ] The goal: Random Corner becomes known within the local creative scene

---

## What Success Looks Like at Day 90

- Regular jam nights running — people expect them
- A small but genuine creative community has formed around the space
- Studio visits are happening — collectors and curious visitors have been inside
- The Instagram tells a coherent story of a living cultural environment
- At least one private event has been hosted or is in conversation

*Financial growth follows cultural credibility. Build the culture first.*
