# 3-Day Sprint

*The point of this sprint is simple: break the inertia. Start moving.*

---

## Day 1 — Space + Presence

### Morning — The Studio
- [ ] Walk the space as a visitor would. What do you notice first?
- [ ] Identify 3–5 strongest artworks — these anchor the room
- [ ] Remove anything unfinished, cluttered, or out of place
- [ ] Check lighting on key pieces

### Afternoon — Digital
- [ ] Instagram bio: *"Artist studio. Music. Marbella."* + location tag
- [ ] Post 3 things: one finished artwork, one process shot, one short video of the space
- [ ] Simple website or Linktree if nothing exists yet — concept, contact, location is enough

### Evening — Outreach
- [ ] Write personal messages to 20 people — not a group blast, individual texts
- [ ] Message: *"We're activating the studio. Come by Thursday evening. Bring someone interesting."*

---

## Day 2 — Thursday Night Setup

### Morning
- [ ] Confirm 2–3 musicians who will actually show up
- [ ] Buy drinks: a case of wine, a case of beer — keep it simple, not a bar setup
- [ ] Set the time: 8pm start works well

### Afternoon
- [ ] Test the PA / music setup
- [ ] Clear floor space so musicians and visitors can mix naturally — no stage separation
- [ ] The room should feel like a studio that music happens to be in, not a venue

### Evening
- [ ] Post one Instagram story: *"Thursday. Studio. Music. Private gathering."*
- [ ] Follow up personally with the 20 people from Day 1

---

## Day 3 — First Jam Night

### Before (6–7pm)
- [ ] Space is ready
- [ ] Drinks are set up casually — not a bar counter, just present
- [ ] At least 2 musicians confirmed

### During
- [ ] Take candid photos and short video clips (30–60 seconds)
- [ ] Introduce people to each other deliberately — this is part of your job as host
- [ ] Don't worry about numbers: 10–15 engaged people is a strong first night

### After
- [ ] Post one photo or reel the same night or next morning
- [ ] Message 5 people who didn't come: *"You missed it. Next Thursday."*

---

## What This Achieves

By end of Day 3:

- The first Jam Night has happened — the 90-day plan has begun
- Social content exists — anyone who searches finds something real
- 10–20 people have been inside the space — word of mouth starts here
- The hardest barrier — starting — is broken
