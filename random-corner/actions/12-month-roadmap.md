# 12-Month Roadmap

*A loose map, not a fixed plan. The space will tell you what it needs.*

---

## Months 1–3 — Foundation
*(See [`90-day-plan.md`](./90-day-plan.md) for detail)*

- Studio activated and presentable
- Thursday Jam Nights running consistently
- Website and Instagram live
- First collectors and visitors inside the space

---

## Months 3–6 — Cultural Credibility

The space starts to have a reputation — people who haven't been have heard of it.

- Expand cultural gatherings beyond just jam nights
- Begin inviting known collectors and cultural figures intentionally
- Refine the studio environment based on what's working
- First artwork sales through studio visits

---

## Months 6–9 — Programming

Random Corner begins to feel like a place with a cultural identity.

- Organize the first curated art preview evening
- Develop collaborations with 1–2 local artists
- Consider a small themed exhibition or special night
- Instagram content tells a consistent, compelling story

---

## Months 9–12 — Institutional Reach

The project has enough identity to present itself to institutions.

- Evaluate cultural partnership opportunities — Ayuntamiento de Marbella, Finnish Cultural Institute in Madrid
- Explore grant applications for specific project-based initiatives
- Consider whether artist residencies or exchange programs make sense
- Review the financial scenarios — which one are you living in?

---

## Horizon — Year 2 and Beyond

When the foundation is solid, these become possible:

- Artist residencies
- Curated exhibition programs
- Nordic-Spanish cultural exchange projects
- Integration into Andalusian and European cultural funding ecosystems
- Random Corner as a recognized institution within Marbella's creative landscape

---

> *"Rather than scaling rapidly, the project is designed to grow organically through relationships, audience development, and cultural credibility."*
